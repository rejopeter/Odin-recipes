Marry Me Quiche

Description

Marry Me Quiche is a delightful and savory dish that combines eggs, cheese, and a variety of vegetables in a flaky pie crust. It's perfect for brunch or a light dinner, and is sure to impress your guests with its rich flavors and creamy texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your choice)
  • 1 cup cooked and chopped vegetables (spinach, bell peppers, onions, or mushrooms)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the pre-made pie crust in a 9-inch pie dish and prick the bottom with a fork to prevent bubbling.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika until well combined.
  4. Spread the cooked and chopped vegetables evenly over the pie crust.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of the egg mixture.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the quiche is set and the top is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  9. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Enjoy your Marry Me Quiche!
